General hints to get started:

  • Klick on the menu on the upper right corner to set up users, teams and shifts. Choose 'Global settings' to see and change the company information.
  • Define when your day starts and ends in the global settings, to avoid white spaces in the calendar when the office is closed. The default setting is from 00.00 h - 24:00 h

How to use the Planning-section:

The month calendar is the default page when signing in. You can add, change, copy and delete events here once you have users set up

  • Click on the or button to change the month, or to jump to the current month.
  • Click on a day to select it (it zooms out to see the events better). When the day is selected, you can either choose to add a new event, or click to copy the events of a day.
  • Click on an event ('color-bar') to view or edit it.
  • Click on the Agent/Manager names to see an hour summary of the current month
  • Only Agents and Managers have a line in the planning, not Admins or Visitors

How does the Coaching-section work?:
