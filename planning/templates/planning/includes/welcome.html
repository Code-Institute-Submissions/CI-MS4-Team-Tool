Thanks for signing up. If you are new to 'EggBox' we advise you to read the following tips:

General hints to get started:

  • Klick on the menu on the upper right corner to set up users, teams and shifts. Choose 'global settings' to view and edit the company information.
  • Define the start- and end time of a working day in the global settings, to avoid white spaces in the calendar when your company is closed. The default setting is from 00.00 h - 24:00 h
  • You can upgrade your plan in the 'global settings' in order to add more team and users than in the free plan

The Planning Section

The month-overview is the default page when signing in. You can add, change, copy and delete events here once you have users set up.

  • Click on the or button to change the month, or to jump to the current month.
  • Click on a day to select it (zoom to see the day better).
  • When the day is selected, you can either choose to add a new event, or click to copy the events of a day
  • Click on an event ('color-bar') to view or edit it.
  • Click on a user name in the left column of the table to see a summary of the selected month
  • Only Agents and Managers get a line in the planning, not Admins or Visitors

The Coaching Section

  • A manager can meet with an Agent or organize a coaching session, and define goals
  • Every Agent has a main goal in the settings as a guideline.

Notifications

  • The 'Messages'-section shows Notifications if another user has added/changed/deleted an event
